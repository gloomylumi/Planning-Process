Project Guide


What is the purpose of this site?

Thefigleaffurniture.com will become the primary mode of advertising for The Fig Leaf Furniture storefront (via promotional avenues like Yelp and GoogleAdWords). The site should serve as a visual representation of—as well as an invitation to—our store. Through strategic use of color and texture, we want to create an experience that is evocative of the cozy feeling of being at home. Quality product images and a layout that effectively showcases is critical to a successful design. Ultimately, the site should elicit a curiosity that can only be fulfilled by coming into the store for a tactile experience.


Who is the target audience?

Our target demographic is 35-65 years old with household income $65k+ and favors traditional or transitional interior design aesthetics over purely contemporary aesthetics.


What are the functional requirements?

Mobile friendly, responsive design. Easily view/browse photos of the showroom. Link to external brand websites. Form for gathering customer information (e-mail blasts).


Who is in charge of acquiring all the content?

Ari Clark is responsible for all media content. Written content is a collaborative effort between Rob Shotwell, Laura Shotwell, and Ari Clark.
